Kingdom Of Dreams

The Kingdom of Dreams, also known as KOD sits majestically in Gurugram,India. It offers visitors a taste of contemporary and modern India through the means of interactive tableaus, galleys, and arts. Inside the Kingdom of Dreams, Culture Gully, India's first-ever skydome has stalls capturing the culture of fourteen different states with local fine arts, cuisines and street arts.
